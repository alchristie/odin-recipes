The perfect spaghetti recipe to guarantee his soul's escaping through this hole that is gaping.


Ingredients

  • One box of dried spaghetti noodles
  • Water
  • One half can of expired tomato sauce
  • 6oz chicken cutlets

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, bring water to a boil and add spaghetti noodles
  2. Empty the can of expired tomato sauce into a microwave safe bowl and microwave on low for 12 minutes or until pasta reaches al dente
  3. Add chicken cutlets to an oiled pan at medium-high heat and cook for 30 seconds on each side, or until internal temperature reaches 75 degrees Fahrenheit

  6. You only get one shot, do not miss your chance to blow
  7. This opportunity comes once in a lifetime, yo
  8. strain the cooked pasta and transfer it to the desired serving dish. Pour the sauce over the spaghetti and garnish with chicken cutlets
